How to get here

Year-round regular flights to Athens are available through Olympic Air and Aegean Airlines

Airlines to Athens. Most international flights to Corfu are connected through Athens. EasyJet also provides direct flights from London Gatwick, Bristol, Manchester and Berlin Schoenefeld. AirSea Lines – Pegasus Aviation, a Greek seaplane operator, offers scheduled flights from Corfu to other Greek islands such as Paxos, Lefkada, Patras and Ithaca.

AirSea Lines – Pegasus Aviation

During the holiday season (May/June to September) airlines offer additional weekly charter flights, including destinations in Germany, United Kingdom, Austria, Switzerland, Norway, Czech Republic and Hungary. The flight times are between 2 and 4 hours.

Tirana Airport, Albania: Kep Merli is also accessible through Tirana International Airport. Direct flights from Ancona, Athens, Bari, Belgrade, Bergamo, Bologna, Brussels, Frankfurt, Genoa, Istanbul, Ljubjana, London, Milan, Munich, Pisa, Roma, Turin, Venice, Verona and Vienna are available through this aiport, all of which last less than 3 hours.After completion of all scheduled road improvements, this time is expected to be two and half hours. Sea

Boat connections from Corfu Island or Iogoumenitsa mainland to Saranda (and thus Kep Merli) are numerous.
Corfu Harbour – Saranda Ferries run on a daily basis making the transfer quite comfortable. The journey by Speed Boat takes 25 minutes, while the journey by the car-carrier Slow Boat takes 1 hour and 20 minutes. Reservation in advance is necessary for cars.
Igoumenitsa - Kep Merli The Igoumenitsa ferry harbour is accessible through direct boats from Paxi, Patra, Ancona, Bari, Brindisi, Venice etc. Thereafter a 30 minute car drive leads you to Kep Merli.
